                        <h1>Rampion Kids Part Three</h1>
                        <div class="postDate">Posted on <a href="http://aplacebetweendoors.tumblr.com/post/139280426756/rampion-kids-part-three">February 14, 2016</a>  with 230 notes <span><a href="http://nothingtoseehere-move-along.tumblr.com/post/139266326322/rampion-kids-part-three" title="nothingtoseehere-move-along" target="_blank">via</a> / <a href="http://nothingtoseehere-move-along.tumblr.com/post/139266326322/rampion-kids-part-three" title="nothingtoseehere-move-along" target="_blank">source</a></span></div>
                        <div class="postBody">
                            <p><a href="http://nothingtoseehere-move-along.tumblr.com/post/139266326322/rampion-kids-part-three" class="tumblr_blog" target="_blank">nothingtoseehere-move-along</a>:</p>

<blockquote><h2><b>Kaider:</b></h2><ul><li>For Kai and Cinder, having kids was important to insure that the royal line continued, but to be honest, they were both terrified by the idea at first. Neither of them had any experience with children, but they figure if they survived a revolution they can probably survive children. Maybe. Hopefully.<br></li><li>Crown Princess Ember is the first to come along, about three years into their marriage.</li><ul><li>Her name was Kai&rsquo;s idea, as a nod to Cinder&rsquo;s nonofficial name, and he stubbornly refused to pick a more traditional choice, so they went with it.</li><li>Given that her parents share many of the same traits, it&rsquo;s hard to say exactly which side she inherited her sharp, intelligent brown eyes and tan skin from, but that wickedly sardonic half-smile is <i>completely</i> Cinder&rsquo;s and the contagious laughter that spills out of her when her brother makes a sassy remark and her thick, black hair are unmistakeably Kai&rsquo;s.</li><li>From the moment she&rsquo;s old enough to understand that she&rsquo;s going to inherit the crown someday, Ember is <i>ecstatic</i> about it. She&rsquo;s going to get her daddy&rsquo;s job! She&rsquo;s going to get to help people and write pretty speeches and dance the first dance at the Peace Festival and she absolutely <i>cannot wait</i>.</li><ul><li>She shadows both her parents while they work every chance she gets. She&rsquo;s spent many a meeting sitting in her mom&rsquo;s lap while Cinder works and many a speech balanced on her father&rsquo;s hip while the cameras flash to catch the cutest photos ever.</li></ul><li>That dedication never goes away. She throws herself into her studies and even does extra work to insure that she&rsquo;s the most knowledgeable person ever in politics and law(both national and international) and diplomacy and everything else that could possibly make her a better ruler for her people. It&rsquo;s all she&rsquo;s ever wanted to do and she intends to be awesome at it.</li></ul><li>Approximately two years later, Prince Rento comes along.</li><ul><li>Rento is absolutely the spitting image of his father, from his lanky frame to his shaggy hair. He even copies some of his father&rsquo;s mannerisms (which Cinder finds hilarious and which always reminds Torin of Kai and Rikkan).</li><li>That being said, there&rsquo;s an awful lot of his mother in him, too. He doesn&rsquo;t suffer fools well <i>at all</i> and has gotten more than a few lectures from Surrogate Grandpa Torin about not sassing the press when they say stupid things <strike>though Cinder may or may not be giving him secret high fives whenever he pulls off a particularly great burn</strike>.</li><li>Unlike his sister, Rento has no interest whatsoever in his royal responsibilities. In fact, he spends a lot of his time jokingly asking if maybe they can dissolve this monarchy like Mom dissolved hers.</li><ul><li>Ember always throws her shoe(or whatever&rsquo;s closest) at him when he does that.</li></ul><li>Rento would much rather spend his time working on his music. Like most young royals, he was given the requisite vocal and instrumental training, but unlike most, he fell head over heels in love with music.</li><ul><li>He sings duets with Aunt Cress whenever the Rampion is in town.</li><li>He&rsquo;s even released a song or two online under a false name and gained a bit of a following for them.</li></ul></ul></ul></blockquote>

<h2><b>2016 YA Reads by Authors of Color</b></h2>
<p>*more titles will be added once more covers are revealed*</p>
<p><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F25387905-american-ace%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=YjNjNmQ5YTM1MWY1NDI3YTE2ZGQzYTU0NjkzNGI1ZWJlYmNkNTEyYSxZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank"><b>American Ace by Marilyn Nelson</b></a> -&nbsp;Connor&rsquo;s grandmother leaves his dad a letter when she dies, and the letter&rsquo;s confession shakes their tight-knit Italian-American family: The man who raised Dad is not his birth father.But the only clues to this birth father&rsquo;s identity are a class ring and a pair of pilot&rsquo;s wings. And so Connor takes it upon himself to investigate.</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F25541365-the-black-disc%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=ODI5NTA0Y2QwNzExZjBmYzE0Mjc0Mjg4Yjc5MDFkZjZmMTM4ZTVhZSxZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Black Disc by David Ramirez</a></b> -&nbsp;On the surface, 17-year-old Susan King is a normal girl with normal problems - but Susan has a secret online life as a hacktivist, using the internet to bring down corrupt corporations around the world. Until the day a black disc appears from nowhere and begins to orbit the earth.&nbsp;That same day, Susan - and millions of people around the globe - receive an incoherent chain email, full of lunatic predictions claiming to foretell the end of the world. Susan, and everyone else, ignores the email. And then the predictions start coming true. But what can one girl do to stop the apocalypse?</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F25982606-burn-baby-burn%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=MzJlMzBlMzUwNjEwNDhhYzQ0MzEyYzllNWM3YmQ3Y2M4YjQ4MGMzMixZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Burn Baby Burn by Med Medina</a></b> -&nbsp;Nora Lopez is 17 during the infamous New York summer of 1977, when the city is besieged by arson, a massive blackout, and a serial killer named Son of Sam is on the loose. All Nora wants is to turn eighteen and be on her own. And while there is a cute new guy who started working with her at the deli, is dating even worth the risk when the killer likes picking off couples who stay out too late? &nbsp;</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F25812632-consider%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=OTRlNTM1YWZkNzY0MTk4MDc3ZTE3YWFmOWZkZTM5YjA5NjAwNWVmMyxZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Consider by Kristy Acevedo</a></b> -&nbsp;As if Alexandra Lucas&rsquo; anxiety disorder isn&rsquo;t enough, mysterious holograms suddenly appear from the sky, heralding the end of the world. They bring an ultimatum: heed the warning and step through a portal-like vertex to safety, or stay and be destroyed by a comet they say is on a collision course with earth. How&rsquo;s that for senior year stress?The holograms, claiming to be humans from the future, bring the promise of safety. But without the ability to verify their story, Alex is forced to consider what is best for her friends, her family, and herself.To stay or to go. A decision must be made.<br></p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F26156203-the-crown-s-game%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=ZjJhNmZjMjk3NDVjNjg1YzcyNmIyNjdhNGVlMjE1ODUyZmRmMTZiNixZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">The Crown&rsquo;s Game by Evelyn Skye </a></b>-&nbsp;Vika Andreyeva can summon the snow and turn ash into gold. Nikolai Karimov can see through walls and conjure bridges out of thin air. They are enchanters&mdash;the only two in Russia&mdash;and with the Ottoman Empire and the Kazakhs threatening, the Tsar needs a powerful enchanter by his side.And so he initiates the Crown&rsquo;s Game, an ancient duel of magical skill&mdash;the greatest test an enchanter will ever know. The victor becomes the Imperial Enchanter and the Tsar&rsquo;s most respected adviser. The defeated is sentenced to death. When Pasha, Nikolai&rsquo;s best friend and heir to the throne, also starts to fall for Vika, Nikolai must defeat the girl they both love&hellip;or be killed himself. As long-buried secrets emerge, threatening the future of the empire, it becomes dangerously clear &hellip; the Crown&rsquo;s Game is not one to lose.</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F26625720-the-darkest-hour%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=MGMyNDYxYzQ2MmU2YzgzYjA0NDAxMzljYzU2NGJhOGQ2NzU5ZWE3OCxZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">The Darkest Hour by Caroline Tung Richmond</a></b> -&nbsp;After the Nazis killed her brother on the North African front, 16-year-old Lucie Blaise volunteered at the Office of Strategic Services in Washington, DC, to do my part for the war effort. Only instead of a desk job at the OSS, Lucie is tapped to join the Clandestine Operations &ndash; a secret espionage and sabotage organization of girls, and employed to German-occupied France to gather intelligence and eliminate Nazi targets. Her current mission: Track down and interrogate a Nazi traitor about a weapon that threatens to wipe out all of Western Europe.&nbsp;</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=My+name+is+Lucie+Blaise.++I+am+sixteen+years+old.++I+have+many+aliases%2C+but+I+am+none+of+the+girls+you+see.++What+I+am+is+the+newest+agent+of+the+CO-7.++And+we+are+here+to+take+down+Hitler.++After+the+Nazis+killed+my+brother+on+the+North+African+front%2C+I+volunteered+at+the+Office+of+Strategic+Services+in+Washington%2C+DC%2C+to+do+my+part+for+the+war+effort.+Only+instead+of+a+desk+job+at+the+OSS%2C+I+was+tapped+to+join+the+Clandestine+Operations+--+a+secret+espionage+and+sabotage+organization+of+girls.+Six+months+ago%2C+I+was+deployed+to+German-occupied+France+to+gather+intelligence+and+eliminate+Nazi+targets.++My+current+mission%3A+Track+down+and+interrogate+a+Nazi+traitor+about+a+weapon+that+threatens+to+wipe+out+all+of+Western+Europe.+Then+find+and+dismantle+the+weapon+before+Hitler+detonates+it.+But+the+deeper+I+infiltrate%2C+the+more+danger+I%27m+in.+Because+the+fate+of+the+free+world+hangs+in+the+balance%2C+and+trusting+the+wrong+person+could+cause+millions+of+lives+to+be+lost.+Including+my+own.&amp;t=MDczMjJmMGMzN2FkYzI4YjdlY2Y2ZWYzYmViZTUzMGI3NWE3ZWEyYixZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Dove Exiled (Dove Chronicles #2) by Karen Bao</a></b> -&nbsp;Phaet Theta fled the Moon and has been hiding on Earth with her friend Wes and his family. But Phaet&rsquo;s past catches up with her when the Lunar Bases attack the community and reveal that Phaet is a fugitive. She&rsquo;s torn between staying on Earth with Wes&mdash;whom she&rsquo;s just discovered her feelings for&mdash;and stowing away on a Moon-bound ship to rescue her siblings.</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F22060619-enter-title-here%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=NWVmM2NkNTM2OGQ0ZDg1MjhhODZmYzZjODNlNmEzOTJmMWNjMmJhNixZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Enter Title Here by Rahul Kanakia</a></b> -&nbsp;

Reshma Kapoor is a college counselor&rsquo;s dream, but if Reshma wants to get into Stanford, and into med school after that, she needs the hook to beat them all.&nbsp;What&rsquo;s a habitual over-achiever to do? Land herself a literary agent, of course. Which is exactly what Reshma does. Her agent wants to represent Reshma, and with scoring a book deal, Reshma knows she&rsquo;ll finally have the key to Stanford.&nbsp;But she&rsquo;s convinced no one would want to read a novel about a study machine like her.&nbsp;Of course, even with a mastermind like Reshma in charge, things can&rsquo;t always go as planned. And when the valedictorian spot begins to slip, she&rsquo;ll have to decide just how far she&rsquo;ll go for that satisfying ending. (Note: It&rsquo;s pretty far.)

</p>
<p><b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F24218983-even-if-the-sky-falls%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=Mzk1ZjhkZmNiZmNmNzlmMjE3NmVkZGJkNWViNzAxZTk2YWMxMjc3NixZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Even If The Sky Falls by Mia Garcia</a></b> -&nbsp;Julie is desperate for a change. So she heads to New Orleans with her youth group to rebuild houses and pretend her life isn&rsquo;t a total mess. In a moment of daring, she ditches her work and heads straight into the heart of Mid-Summer Mardi Gras, where she locks eyes with Miles, an utterly irresistible guy with a complicated story of his own. She jumps at the chance to see the real New Orleans, and in one surreal night, they fall in love. But their adventure takes an unexpected turn when an oncoming hurricane changes course. As the storm gains power and Julie is pulled back into chaos she finds pretending everything is fine is no longer an option.</p>
<p>

<b><a href="http://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodreads.com%2Fbook%2Fshow%2F24846331-ever-the-hunted%3Ffrom_search%3Dtrue%26search_version%3Dservice&amp;t=MTg4MWMwZWI2NDMyNjIxOTM3Mzc4MTAyM2MxOGRkYjI1NzZkNDNlOSxZMmpJd3BxTg%3D%3D" target="_blank">Ever the Hunted by Erin Summerill </a></b>- 17-year-old Britta Flannery is at ease only in the woods with her dagger and bow. She spends her days tracking criminals alongside her father, the legendary bounty hunter for the King of Malam&mdash;that is, until her father is murdered. When Britta is caught poaching by the royal guard, instead of facing the noose she is offered a deal: her freedom in exchange for her father&rsquo;s killer. However, it&rsquo;s not so simple.The alleged killer is none other than Cohen McKay, her father&rsquo;s former apprentice. The only friend she&rsquo;s ever known. The boy she once loved who broke her heart.
