Welcome, Class of 2019

   [14]Allied in Pride

     * [16]Events/
     * [17]E-Board/
     * [18]Queer Guide/
     * [19]Support/

   Through advocacy, social events, service, and support for lesbian, gay,
   bisexual, transgender and queer/questioning students (LGBTQ), we aim to
   create a comfortable environment for GW's LGBTQ population, to raise
   awareness on LGBTQ issues at GW and in the DC Metro Community, and to
   promote pro-LGBTQ policies at the university level.

 Between planning events, hosting speakers, and promoting awareness of LGBTQ
issues, Allied brings together the queer community at GW.
