Hello and welcome...

   ...to my web site! The coming year will mark my 40th year of breeding
   pythons. In that time I've been lucky enough to keep and breed many
   species of pythons and pioneer the production of many color and pattern
   variants of those species. I'll share articles, pictures and videos
   here as well as offer animals for sale.

   [17]Big Retic I intend for the web site to be a dynamic, evolving
   source of information and entertainment, at least for the people that
   share my interest in and passion for pythons! Please come back often!
