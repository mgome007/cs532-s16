          <p><p>Being an actor is one of the most exciting and thrilling occupations in the world. It is also one of the most competitive. Regardless of whether you are just starting out in this occupation or whether you are already have some experience, writing a good actor resume is a must. <p><p>An actor resume template can help you to get started in writing an attention grabbing resume. One thing that you need to keep in mind when using an actor resume template is that actor resumes do tend to be somewhat different from traditional resumes. For example, information that you would never include on a traditional resume will often be included on an actor resume template. Such information may include height and weight as well as eye color and hair color. The reason such information is included on an actor resume template is that it could be critical to a particular role. <p><p>As is the case with any other resume, an actor resume template will also requite you to list information about your past experience. Listing your work experience in a chronological fashion is a good way to list all of your performances. <p><p>In addition to your work experience, an actor resume template will also require that you list any special accomplishments or awards that you have won. Listing such accomplishments and awards can make a big difference in helping you to land a role that you want. <p><p>One of the best ways to present your work experience and your accomplishments on your resume is with bullet points. This helps to draw the reader's eye to the most critical elements of your resume and really helps your resume to stand out. <p><p>Of course, training and education are important elements of any resume as well and that is no different when writing an actor's resume. You should include any special training that you have had to help you with your acting career. This is particularly true for specialized forms of training, such as acting lessons or training. <p></p>
