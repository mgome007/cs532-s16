   Next Phase

   Arnhem
   NextPhaseRecords’s avatar

   Robbert “Infest” Peperkamp and Leon “Leonux” Switzer join forces to
   create Next Phase Records, an exciting new label that aims to release
   an eclectic mix of breakbeat driven music unrestricted by BPM. An
   impressive group of like minded musicians from around the world have
   helped to create the sound and direction of Next Phase with an already
   stunning catalogue of tracks. Based in Arnhem, the Netherlands, Next
   Phase will release a diverse mix of soulful underground sounds
   complemented by specially commissioned art and bold graphic design. The
   vision is clear, timeless music and art as one, something that speaks
   to your senses. Music without boundaries. A Next Phase release could
   contain Soul, Jazz, Hip Hop or Jungle. Music should be challenging and
   fresh to the ear, packed with drum heat and emotion. With ten years of
   experience in the Dutch Jungle/DNB scene, Leon and Robbert host a
   fortnightly radio-show on www.jungletrain.net Contact and bookings:
   info@nextphaserecords.com
