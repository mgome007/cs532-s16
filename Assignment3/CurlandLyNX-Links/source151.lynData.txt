   Christina Rollo is a fine art nature photographer who was born and
   raised in central New York. She enjoys exploring the natural areas that
   surround her and spends most of her time outdoors. Christina's passion
   for animals has led her to take many photos of wild animals, pets,
   nature and scenery, as well as a variety of other subjects.
   Christina's fine art prints are held in public and private collections
   throughout the world. She has hundreds of photos and artworks to choose
   from in her on-line galleries. All of her fine art prints can be
   purchased ready-to-hang in a variety of sizes and mediums and every
   purchase includes a [118]money-back guarantee. You have nothing to
