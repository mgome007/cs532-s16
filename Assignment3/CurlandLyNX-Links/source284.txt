				<h1><a href="http://www.datenteiler.de/install-freedos-1-1-with-freedosmultiboot/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Install FreeDOS 1.1 with FreeDOSmultiboot">Install FreeDOS 1.1 with FreeDOSmultiboot</a></h1>
					
				<div class="descr">2. Oktober 2015 von Christian Imhorst</div>

				<div class="entry">

					<p>If you want to install FreeDOS to your hard drive with the help of FreeDOSmultiboot on a pen drive you should first know, what you are doing: You need a backup, you can destroy your data and you can damage your hardware.</p>
<p>THIS SOFTWARE COMES WITH ABSOLUTELY NO WARRANTY!<br />
USE AT YOUR OWN RISK!</p>
<p>Then you need to download and unzip <a href="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/FreeDOSmultiboot.zip">FreeDOSmultiboot.zip</a> [1] and copy FreeDOSmultiboot.img to your pen drive. On Windows use <a href="http://sourceforge.net/projects/win32diskimager/" target="_blank">Win32 DiskImager</a> [2] or <a href="https://rufus.akeo.ie/" target="_blank">Rufus</a> [3] or similar. On Unix based systems like Linux you can use the <code>dd</code> command as superuser/root:</p>

<div class="wp_syntax"><table><tr><td class="code"><pre class="bash" style="font-family:monospace;"><span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">dd</span> <span style="color: #007800;">if</span>=FreeDOSmultiboot.img <span style="color: #007800;">of</span>=<span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">/</span>dev<span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">/</span>sdX</pre></td></tr></table></div>

<p>Replace X with the letter of the device name of your pen drive.</p>
<p>Boot your device from your prepared pen drive.  Choose &#8222;Install FreeDOS 1.1&#8220; from the boot menu to start the installer.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-10-02-22-47-55.png"><img src="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-10-02-22-47-55-300x167.png" alt="Bildschirmfoto vom 2015-10-02 22-47-55" width="300" height="167" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-9797" srcset="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-10-02-22-47-55-300x167.png 300w, http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-10-02-22-47-55.png 720w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>If needed choose &#8222;Create Drive C:&#8220; in the installer menu.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-03-47.png"><img src="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-03-47-300x176.png" alt="Bildschirmfoto vom 2015-09-29 21-03-47" width="300" height="176" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-9799" srcset="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-03-47-300x176.png 300w, http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-03-47.png 720w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>Maybe you have to delete a Non-DOS partiton on your computer to create a new &#8222;Primary DOS Partiton&#8220; for FreeDOS installation. </p>
<p><a href="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-29-08.png"><img src="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-29-08-300x176.png" alt="Bildschirmfoto vom 2015-09-29 21-29-08" width="300" height="176" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-9801" srcset="http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-29-08-300x176.png 300w, http://www.datenteiler.de/wp-content/uploads/2015/10/Bildschirmfoto-vom-2015-09-29-21-29-08.png 720w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>If the DOS partition is created you have to format the partition. Please note to set the C drive on the harddisk as active.</p>
<p>After a new reboot, you have to boot from the pen drive again, choose &#8222;Install FreeDOS 1.1&#8220; from the boot menu to start the installer. Then you can choose &#8222;Install to harddisk&#8220; from the menu and proceed with the FreeDOS installation.</p>
<p>There are more detailed information about the installation in the <a href="http://freedos.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/VirtualBox" target="_blank">FreeDOS Install HOWTO</a> [3]. This is for VirtualBox, but should apply to any PC.</p>
<p>Happy FreeDOS&#8217;ing.</p>
