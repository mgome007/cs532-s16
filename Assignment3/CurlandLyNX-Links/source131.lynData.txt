Who we are

   WeAreTheCity Jobs is the careers section of leading UK women's website
   WeAreTheCity.com.
   Our aim is to encourage more talented women to pursue their career
   aspirations by featuring profiles of inspirational role models, career
   guidance, and a jobs board with open roles from our amazing clients.
   Due to the limited number of women in the pipeline in many firms our
   emphasis is on attracting women to apply for these roles, but please
   note that all of these roles are open to men too.
