<h2 class='date-header'><span>Wednesday, June 4, 2014</span></h2>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>
<meta content='http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/Up8mvSeDRpMJocbW6kN1bw/cid/122561065/id/rpSP_VTg4xGeal-FO-wIHA/size/c600x404.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.skgiffard.com/2014/06/my-favorite-things-for-may-june.html'>My Favorite Things for May & June</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4668466865102090125' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="margin: 0 auto; width: 550px;">
<div style="position: relative;">
<a href="http://www.polyvore.com/favorite_things_for_may/set?.embedder=2774295&amp;.svc=copypaste&amp;id=122561065" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img alt="Favorite Things For May" border="0" src="http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/Up8mvSeDRpMJocbW6kN1bw/cid/122561065/id/rpSP_VTg4xGeal-FO-wIHA/size/c600x404.jpg" height="404" title="Favorite Things For May" width="550" /></span></a></div>
</div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">The weather is finally getting warmer and outdoor markets are popping up around Boston faster than the flowers! I'm still running around (now on purpose) and trying to soak in as much of the spring as possible.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00D9NRCFG/ref=oh_details_o00_s00_i00?ie=UTF8&amp;psc=1"><img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-34Kn216ldTU/U3uwWmUAY8I/AAAAAAAAASI/lmnj4FEI1dw/s1600/neewege+running+pack+fanny+pack+running+belt+run+sports+athetics+skgiffard+jog+iphone+ipod+race+marathon.jpg" /></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><a href="http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00D9NRCFG/ref=oh_details_o00_s00_i00?ie=UTF8&amp;psc=1">1. Neewer // Running Waist Bag&nbsp;</a></span></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I typically don't run with a phone. I like being able to run without my technological ball and chain. That being said, I recently decided to get back into running. To take my mind off how out of shape I felt I started listening to podcasts on my iPhone. I am a finicky runner so much so that having anything in my hand or on my arm throws me off balance. I LOVE this belt from Neewer. I can barely tell its on, it holds my phone and my keys and its water resistant. I actually have no idea what I look with it on, but the who goal of exercise is to make yourself look like a fool in the most exhausting way possible, right?</span></span></div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; text-align: center;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif; text-align: center;"><br /></span>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.pandora.com/station/543276048370435760" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-W-EquONmJqE/U3u2QCK6_4I/AAAAAAAAASQ/9jbryEnK6Io/s1600/Louis+Armstrong+La+Vie+En+Rose.jpg" height="320" width="320" /></span></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.pandora.com/station/543276048370435760"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">2. My "La Vie en Rose" Station on Pandora</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I first heard La Vie en Rose when I was watching <a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0047437/">Sabrina</a>&nbsp;staring Audrey Hepburn. The song has been one of my favorite songs ever since. The playlist based on this song is one of my favorites to listen to when I am lounging on the porch or cooking a fun dinner.&nbsp;&nbsp;In addition to the Louis Armstrong version of La Vie En Rose, there are songs by Frank Sinatra, Ella Fitzgerald, Bobby Darin and a bunch of non-lyrical french songs that I love.</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="https://www.birkenstockusa.com/products/women/sandals/gizeh/black-birko-flor/4369"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-5bGzwu_6Bc4/U3vSGJDndhI/AAAAAAAAASc/SluOOXuFP5Q/s1600/birkenstock+birks+cork+snadal+thong+gizeh+flor+comfortable+skgiffard+hippie+prep+preppy.jpg" height="175" width="320" /></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.birkenstockusa.com/products/women/sandals/gizeh/black-birko-flor/4369"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">3. Birkenstock // Gizeh&nbsp;</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Until about a month ago I thought of Birkenstocks as sandals for hippies and people with foot problems. I was right! But I finally tried them on and they are rarely off my feet. These are actually kind of cute and stylish. Do your feet a favor and ditch your normal pavement slappers for these cork cruisers. If anyone pokes fun, just tell them I made you do it.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="https://www.cuisinart.com/products/blenders/cpb-300pk.html"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-OnBwBEJHqWs/U40fZCBL1CI/AAAAAAAAASw/bWy4CENSKQE/s1600/cusinart+blender+pink+smoothies+healthy+fruit+kale+spinach+togo+cup+skgiffard.jpg" height="248" width="320" /></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://www.cuisinart.com/products/blenders/cpb-300pk.html"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">4. Cusinart // CPB-300PK Compact Blender</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I am not a very healthy eater. I'm not a huge fan of vegetables, and while I'll eat fruit if it's cut up and prepared, I'd rather snack on carbs in almost any form. Enter this blender. I have been drinking a banana a serving of fruit and kale/spinach every day for the last month or so. These individual cups with lids (lids set them apart) are the perfect vehicles for sneaking the greens into my smoothie is perfect. Sometimes I even throw them into milkshakes. I've also started adding ground up flax seeds which are not only a good source of omega-3 fatty acids, but also provide another way in which I'm turning into my mother.&nbsp;</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="https://baggu.com/shop/standardbaggu/hotpink"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-eUc_6ruQgco/U40gFQ4NPfI/AAAAAAAAAS4/lrpDTrBQgxc/s1600/baggu+resusable+tote+bag+grocery+hot+pink+neon+ecofriendly+environment+prep+preppy+skgiffard.jpg" /></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://baggu.com/shop/standardbaggu/hotpink"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">5. Baggu // Standard in Hot Pink</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I have been carrying reusable bags to the grocery store since I was in college. They carry more than the average grocery bag and are environmentally friendly as well. I like this fold up bag from Baggu because it doesn't take up a lot of space in my work tote, but it's always there in case I need to grab some groceries on the way home.</span></div>

<h2 class='date-header'><span>Sunday, May 18, 2014</span></h2>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>
<meta content='http://photos-e.ak.instagram.com/hphotos-ak-frc/918131_501923186591148_1533359594_n.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.skgiffard.com/2014/05/i-used-to-be-in-shape-once-long-long-ago.html'>I Think I Might Have Been in Shape Once, But I Can&#39;t Seem To Remember
When...</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2532884898671030363' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I was a lifelong athlete, until I graduated college. Growing up I can hardly remember an afternoon that wasn't filled with soccer practices, pool time or laps around the track. I was a high jumper for my first two years of college at Bryant University. I chose not to do track when I transferred to Auburn, but I had no idea what to do with all my free time now that I no longer had hours of track practices. I ended up repeating my summer workouts after my classes to occupy that space, crazy I know.</span><br>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br></span>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I'm not like that anymore. Working out seems like a chore that takes too much time. And going to the gym is a waste of money and they are all crowded etc. etc. etc. Here is where the magic of YouTube comes in because there are 1000s of work out videos that are fun, free and fast. Below are my two of my favorite instructors:

</span><br>
<br>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/lhefez" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Lauren Hefez</span></a></div>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="http://instagram.com/sweatwithlaurenhefez" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: xx-small;"><img alt="from her Instagram" border="0" src="http://photos-e.ak.instagram.com/hphotos-ak-frc/918131_501923186591148_1533359594_n.jpg" height="320" width="320"></span></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: xx-small;"><a href="http://instagram.com/sweatwithlaurenhefez" target="_blank">from her Instagram</a></span></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I think I found Lauren's YouTube videos through Buzzfeed. Her workouts are extremely effective (I had a hard time climbing my stairs after her<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pv9uzcSBPQI&amp;list=PLkvB-KT8eRUV2MeUOXZ2oeSvNJwxBijrK&amp;index=4" target="_blank"> booty shorts workout</a>) and can be done in a small space. She has work outs for both cardio and for muscle building.&nbsp;</span></div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br></span>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<a href="https://www.youtube.com/user/TaraStilesYoga" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Tara Stiles</span></a></div>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="http://instagram.com/tarastiles" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: xx-small;"><img border="0" src="http://photos-b.ak.instagram.com/hphotos-ak-frc/914342_855800871103649_739057404_n.jpg" height="320" width="320"></span></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://instagram.com/tarastiles" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: xx-small;">from her Instagram</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Tara Stiles has yoga for every situation. From <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yv_XK6xV7n0">hip opening sequences</a> to yoga for <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SZoilpgyhCY">hotels</a> and <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5nyBq1tcEL0">hangovers</a>. I like her videos because she makes yoga accessible, if you aren't as in shape as you'd like to be. I will usually string a few of her videos together and end with her <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VS0ou7H2Cd8">ultimate stretch yoga routine</a>&nbsp;because deep down inside I still want to be able to do splits like Shannon Miller.</span></div>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>
<meta content='http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-6EZksxC5Aoo/U3N8Mzen_7I/AAAAAAAAARE/q4u6m1xjNRI/s1600/Barefoot+In+The+Park+Jane+Fona+Robert+Redford+New+York+Preppy+Prep.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.skgiffard.com/2014/05/my-favorite-movies-after-long-week.html'>My Favorite Movies After a Long Week</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-6382906075885979297' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Whenever I've had a really long and exhausting week I like to curl up in bed and put on one of these movies. They are not mentally taxing and they all feature strong female leads juggling the different aspects of their lives. Even though they are movies and real life isn't a movie blah, blah, blah, they give me a little hope that I can keep everything together.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0061385/?ref_=ttmd_md_nm" target="_blank"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-6EZksxC5Aoo/U3N8Mzen_7I/AAAAAAAAARE/q4u6m1xjNRI/s1600/Barefoot+In+The+Park+Jane+Fona+Robert+Redford+New+York+Preppy+Prep.jpg" height="320" width="171" /></a></div>
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0061385/?ref_=ttmd_md_nm" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"></span></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Jane Fonda and a extremely preppy Robert Redford take on marriage and the New York Housing Market</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1570728/?ref_=nv_sr_1" target="_blank"><img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-UP4PKzuhLbU/U3N8bfQV_DI/AAAAAAAAARM/Rj683pm69gw/s1600/crazy+stupid+love+steve+carrell+emma+stone+ryan+gosling+comedy+julianne+moore.jpg" height="320" width="216" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Great actors and well written lines that actually hold some truth. Also Ryan Gossling's abs if you're into that sort of thing.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0457939/?ref_=ttmd_md_nm" target="_blank"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-pJ-z-OUZSg0/U3N8ljEcrVI/AAAAAAAAARU/ZALf3RGOWtM/s1600/the+holiday+cameron+diaz+jack+black+kate+hudson+jude+law+england+la+.jpg" height="320" width="215" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">New places, new loves. Directed by Nancy Meyers and Scored by Hans Zimmer, who both do an amazing job.</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1230414/?ref_=ttmd_md_nm" target="_blank"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-t3AFahSxdls/U3N8votUElI/AAAAAAAAARc/snFPe0pk7ko/s1600/its+complicated+maryl+streep+alec+baldwin+steve+martin.jpg" height="320" width="168" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">I love Meryl. She is a goddess. Also written &nbsp;and directed by&nbsp;Nancy Meyers&nbsp;with music by Hans Zimmer. <a href="http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0583600/?ref_=nv_sr_1" target="_blank">Nancy Meyers </a>&nbsp;can do a rom-com like no other.</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0045891/?ref_=ttmd_md_nm" target="_blank"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-B_yrTxiMKlo/U3N9JD-lPhI/AAAAAAAAARk/K-iYlxjpxyk/s1600/how+to+marry+a+millionaire+marylin+monroe+betty+grable+lauren+bacall.jpg" height="320" width="206" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Marylin Monroe, Betty Grable and Lauren Bacall on the hunt for billionaires. I like that part of this movie is set in Maine, because who doesn't love Maine?&nbsp;<a href="http://www.netflix.com/" target="_blank">On Netflix!</a></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0046250/?ref_=nv_sr_1"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-_4ztqlxLCHk/U3N9gPHkqQI/AAAAAAAAARs/7doTtRtH4aY/s1600/roman+holiday+audrey+hepburn+robert+peck.jpg" height="320" width="182" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Audrey Hepburn as a princess experiencing "normal" life in Rome with Gregory Peck. I wish that was my "normal" life.&nbsp;<a href="http://www.netflix.com/" target="_blank">On Netflix!</a></span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0256415/"><img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-dWQUsYcYiwk/U3N9mqCiSUI/AAAAAAAAAR0/ELXb5bkZmP8/s1600/sweet+home+alabama+dixie+reese+witherspoon+southern+prep.jpg" height="320" width="216" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">&nbsp;Reese Witherspoon doing that whole southern thang. Having lived in Alabama during college, this movie makes me a tad bit homesick.&nbsp;</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.skgiffard.com/2014/04/my-rainboot-picks-for-this-spring.html'>My Rainboot Picks for This Spring</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4270933313668511332' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="margin: 0 auto; width: 550px;">
<div style="position: relative;">
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<a href="http://www.polyvore.com/running_through_rain/set?.embedder=2774295&amp;.svc=copypaste&amp;id=117907525" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img alt="Running Through the Rain" border="0" src="http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/28tQLps9avgWyBDGVKCTw/cid/117907525/id/xgSvn9m44xGI7WnfZFZllw/size/c600x634.jpg" height="634" title="Running Through the Rain" width="550" /></span></a></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Don't let the rain get you down! Here are four styles of rain boots to keep you dry this spring.</span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">CITY</span></div>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://usa.hunter-boot.com/product/sandhurst-savoy-riding-boots" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img alt=" Hunter // Sandhurst Savoy Riding Boots" border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ssZqho6rNxw/Uzrix0qLQeI/AAAAAAAAAMc/Dk6Hg_w5jGo/s1600/Hunter+Sandhurst+Savory+Riding+Boots+rain+quilt+quilted+prep+preppy.jpg" /></span></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://usa.hunter-boot.com/product/sandhurst-savoy-riding-boots" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Hunter // Sandhurst Savoy Riding Boot</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
</div>
</div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">These boots are more polished than the original Hunter Boots, but a little more delicate than the original. None the less, the glossy finish and faux-quilting make these perfect for wearing around the city.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">COUNTRY</span><br />
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="http://www.zappos.com/bogs-classic-glosh-rainboot-green" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-BR_M2_QOI0s/UzsHI6mFUoI/AAAAAAAAAMs/5iH7XpSabEI/s1600/bogs+classic+glosh+rain+boot+green+prep+preppy+farm+country.jpg" height="320" width="235" /></span></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.zappos.com/bogs-classic-glosh-rainboot-green" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Bogs // Classic Glosh Rainboot</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">If you will be mucking it up at all this spring I definitely recommend Bogs. They are easy to put on and actually have treads which will offer some traction on slippery surfaces.&nbsp;</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: large;">CASUAL</span><br />
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><span style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><a href="http://www.joulesusa.com/Women/Equestrian/Evedon/Womens-Bow-Welly/Navy?id=Q_EVEDON|NAVY" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-PtkfghH7xYE/UzswM3o8i9I/AAAAAAAAAM8/D6bQef7T0EU/s1600/Joules+Evedon+bow+welly+rain+boot+prep+preppy+navy+red.jpg" /></span></a></span></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.joulesusa.com/Women/Equestrian/Evedon/Womens-Bow-Welly/Navy?id=Q_EVEDON|NAVY" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">Joules // Evedon Women's Bow Welly</span></a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">I kept seeing these boots all over Pinterest and I'm so glad I finally found them! They have an adorable grosgrain bow on the back and a nice shape. On top of that they have a one year warranty.</span><br />
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">CLASSIC</span><br />
<span id="goog_1419533238"></span><br />
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.llbean.com/llb/shop/35477?feat=506697-GN2&amp;page=women-s-l-l-bean-boots-8" target="_blank"><img border="0" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-kD40HX9Y_-U/UzsxPKci9UI/AAAAAAAAANI/Xe8Jce34SDU/s1600/bean+boot+llbean+prep+preppy+duck+book+gumshoe+maine+classic.jpg" height="286" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="320" /></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.llbean.com/llb/shop/35477?feat=506697-GN2&amp;page=women-s-l-l-bean-boots-8" target="_blank">L.L.Bean // &nbsp;8" Bean Boot</a></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span id="goog_1419533239"></span><span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;">In my mind these are the classic rain boot. In fact they are the classic every boot. I find that Bean boots are easy to throw on and not as hot as traditional rainboot styles.&nbsp;</span></div>

<meta content='http://cfc.polyvoreimg.com/cgi/img-set/.sig/ri71p4HKspZycrxtUrfGg/cid/120097603/id/ugOPx-nK4xGaSOaNDXURzQ/size/c600x550.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.skgiffard.com/2014/04/desperately-seeking-seersucker.html'>Desperately seeking Seersucker</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2884053921716719026' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif;">It's finally here! If you live in the South, seersucker season has arrived. Those who live north of Dixie can break out those lovely stripes, but I prefer to keep my seersucker in storage until Memorial day. If you wear your stripes too early you risk getting it spattered with mud, muck and whatever winter wetness is still lingering around.</span></div>
<div style="margin: 0 auto; width: 550px;">
<div style="position: relative;">
