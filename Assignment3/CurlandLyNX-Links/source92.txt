<h3>WELCOME TO BADIE DESIGNS</h3>
<p>Badie Designs is a creative agency located in Atlanta, Georgia. We specialize in Graphic Design, Illustration, Web Development, and Online Marketing. Badie Designs provides the same quality and expertise as big creative agencies without the big price.</p>
<p>We provide our clients with a great solution that will bring results to their business. Badie Designs is passionate about helping businesses with their creative needs. We get to know each client and work with them every step of the way. Each client gives us an opportunity to learn something new. We have worked with over 100 companies and our creative agency is constantly growing. At Badie Designs we believe the final presentation determines a successful brand.</p>
