<h2>About</h2>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">I have been in the media industry since 2002 when I worked for <a title="BBC South Today" href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b006pfl4" target="_blank">BBC South</a> as a Camera Assistant for their <a title="BBC Music Live Golden Jubilee Celebrations at Poole Park, Dorset" href="http://bop.4t2portfolio.co.uk/culture_community_learning/news/www.boroughofpoole.com/licensing@poole.gov.uk/ref:416/" target="_blank">Music Live</a> production. Since then, my desire to develop within this industry has never faltered. I have worked for other broadcasters such as Teachers TV and for Production companies such as <a title="TUP" href="http://thumbsupproductions.tv/" target="_blank">Thumbs up Productions</a> (<a title="BNTM Living TV" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Britain%27s_Next_Top_Model_%28cycle_2%29" target="_blank">Britain’s Next Top Model</a>) which all came after my Fdg in Broadcast Post Production at <a title="Ravensbourne" href="http://www.ravensbourne.ac.uk/" target="_blank">Ravensbourne</a>. </span><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">I now also work on a freelance basis for London TV companies, travel broadcasters, corporate and individuals.  </span></p>
<p>I pride myself on producing great content that leaves a <em>wow</em> factor with the client and the viewer. My ability to film and edit is also very handy and both have seen video broadcast domestically and around the world.</p>
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5em;">I have also developed in other areas such as event photography and managing websites and these are all areas that will combine well with my video production background. </span></p>
