                       A Red-Rose Chain available now!

   The first nine Toby Daye adventures are now available at North American
      bookstores. Check the [45]Toby Daye series landing page for more
                                information.

                      Pocket Apocalypse available now!

     The fourth InCryptid novel, Pocket Apocalypse, is now available at
   North American bookstores. Check the [46]InCryptid series landing page
   for more information. You can also find several free short stories set
      in the InCryptid universe on the series' [47]Short Stories page.

   09/02/2015:

   Small improvements have been happening site-wide. Check to see if
   Seanan (or Mira) will be coming to a bookstore or convention near you!

   06/21/2015:

   Icons and wallpaper have been added for The Winter Long and Sparrow
   Hill Road, along with a plethora of new Appearances! Have a blast!

   11/10/2014:

   Seanan is hard at work updating the website FAQs, and needs your
   questions! If there's something you've been just burning to know,
   please drop her a line via the contact form, and she'll see about
   getting it added.

   (Please note that FAQs are things like "what is the order of the
   books?" and "where can I buy this album?", not "please explain the
   structural significance of this spoiler in book six.)
